1.\u00a0 Establish daily \u201ccheck-ins\u201d with their manager via phone or video.<\/p>\n

2.\u00a0 Provide communication technology options other than their phone and email, such as video conferencing, like GTM or Zoom.<\/p>\n

3.\u00a0 Establish rules of communications engagement with the team, such as text messages for urgent items.<\/p>\n

4.\u00a0 Provide opportunities to promote social interaction:<\/p>\n

    \n
  • Periodic team video conferencing.<\/li>\n
  • Periodically schedule a \u201cpizza party\u201d \u2013 have pizza delivered to team members at a specific time<\/li>\n
  • Start with short \u201ccatch-ups\u201d for personal life<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

    5.\u00a0 Get periodic feedback.<\/p>\n

      \n
    • Ask: \u201cHow is the remote working situation working out for you so far?\u201d<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

      6.\u00a0 Be a leader.\u00a0Don\u2019t communicate stress and helplessness.\u00a0Make comments like:<\/p>\n

        \n
      • We\u2019ve got this\u201d<\/li>\n
      • This is challenging but I know we can handle it\u201d<\/li>\n
      • Let\u2019s look now for ways to focus on our strengths\u201d<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

        7.\u00a0 Discuss ways our team can be more productive and help the company. Examples could include:<\/p>\n

          \n
        • Focus on ecommerce.\u00a0What could we easily start selling?\u00a0Training webinars?\u00a0 Inexpensive relevant products? Use digital ads for immediate sales.<\/li>\n
        • Sharpen our own saw\u201d with online training.<\/li>\n
        • Develop ROI case studies of current customers to provide to salespeople to help convince prospects.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

          8. Discuss ways the team can add value to current customers.<\/p>\n

            \n
          • Increase interactions with them.<\/li>\n
          • Send them useful information, a relevant book or food.<\/li>\n
          • Listen to their challenges.<\/li>\n
          • Upsell and cross-sell as appropriate.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n
